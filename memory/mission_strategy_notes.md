# Mission Strategy Notes

## Overarching Goal:
To equip district members with tools and skills for Bible study, practice, and evangelism, so they don't face difficulties in these areas.

## 1. Sunday Sermon-Based Weekly Plan:
*   **Goal:** Facilitate deeper understanding and personal application of the Sunday sermon throughout the week, and enable easy sharing within the district.
*   **Strategy Outline:**
    *   Break down Sunday sermon into daily digestible units (QT, morning prayer, reflection).
    *   Include prompts for personal application.
    *   Provide discussion points for district meetings/sharing.

## 2. SNS Mission Strategy:
*   **Goal:** Provide accessible and shareable content for district members to effectively evangelize and spread the Word using social media.
*   **Strategy Outline:**
    *   Create a release strategy for various SNS content types (videos, images, letters, news).
    *   Ensure content is engaging, relevant, and easy to share.
    *   Include guidance on effective usage and conversation starters.
